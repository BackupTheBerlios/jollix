Jollix 0.1.1 features:

  • based on Gentoo Linux
  • LiveCD, to check jollix out safely. No harddisk installation necessary
  • optimized for Athlon-CPUs (but works on all i686 machines).
  • supports nVidia-based (full 3D hardware acceleration) and others (Vesa only so far!) graphic cards
  • kernel: Gentoo 2.4.20-gaming-sources
  • KDE 3.1.1a
  • Winex-cvs 2.2.1
  • play your Half-Life-, Public-Enemy- or Counter-Strike hd-installations!
  • multimedia applications (xmms, mplayer, xine u.a.)
